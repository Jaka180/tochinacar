BYD Atto 3

BYD's global bestseller — the compact SUV that opened 70+ export markets.比亚迪全球出口主力——打开 70 多个海外市场的紧凑型 SUV。

Atto 3 is BYD's most important global compact SUV and one of the first models that made BYD visible to mainstream buyers outside China. It packages the e-Platform 3.0, Blade Battery and a practical crossover body in a format that works for Europe, Southeast Asia, Australia and Latin America. Export-market battery packs, safety equipment and range ratings can differ from China-market data.Atto 3 是比亚迪最重要的全球紧凑型 SUV，也是比亚迪最早让海外主流消费者感知到的车型之一。它把 e-Platform 3.0、刀片电池和实用跨界车身整合到适合欧洲、东南亚、澳洲和拉美的产品形态中。出口市场的电池包、安全配置与续航测试标准可能与中国市场不同。
