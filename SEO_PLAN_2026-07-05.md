## Recurring QA Gate

Before every publish, run:

```bash
node build.js
```

Then verify:
- All sitemap URLs return 200.
- Canonical equals final URL.
- Every index page has one H1 and collection schema.
- Informational pages such as About, Editorial Policy, Contact, Newsletter, and Privacy use page-level schema rather than collection schema.
- Every article has NewsArticle schema.
- Every article exposes related coverage links and the site exposes `/feed.xml`.
- No title is outside 20-75 characters.
- No meta description is outside 40-170 characters.
- No future publication date is emitted.
- No referenced image is missing.
- Browser console has no errors on homepage, `/chinese-car-brands`, `/models`, `/news`, `/policy`, and `/data`.
- `/quote` remains crawlable with `noindex,follow`, is excluded from sitemap, and has no `FAQPage` or `Service` schema.
