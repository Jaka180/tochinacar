Seagull is BYD's small EV price weapon, exported in several markets as Dolphin Mini. Its significance is not performance but cost structure: LFP battery chemistry, compact packaging and high-volume manufacturing make it one of the clearest examples of China's EV affordability advantage. Buyers should not mix the entry price with the longest-range trim, because the 305 km and 405 km versions sit at different price points.海鸥是比亚迪的小型纯电价格武器，在部分海外市场以 Dolphin Mini 名称销售。它的意义不在性能，而在成本结构：磷酸铁锂电池、紧凑封装与大规模制造，让它成为中国电动车价格优势最清晰的样本之一。阅读参数时不应把入门价与最长续航版本混用，因为 305 km 与 405 km 版本处在不同价格档。
