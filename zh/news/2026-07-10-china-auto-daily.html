China Auto Exports Hit New Highs in H1 2026: BYD, Chery, Geely All Surpass 100k Monthly中国车企2026年上半年出口全面突破：比亚迪、奇瑞、吉利单月均超10万辆

BYD hit a record 175,349 overseas units in June (+94.7%), Chery posted 191,062 (+79.7%), and Geely broke 100k for the first time, as China's export boom spreads beyond its traditional duopoly.比亚迪6月海外销量创纪录达175,349辆（+94.7%），奇瑞191,062辆（+79.7%），吉利首破10万辆，中国车企出口正式进入多强并跑新阶段。

By TopChinaCar Editorial作者：TopChinaCar 编辑部 · Published July 10, 2026发布于 2026-07-10 · Compiled from the primary sources cited below基于文末所列一手信源编写

China's auto export surge entered a new structural phase in the first half of 2026: BYD, Chery, and Geely all crossed the 100,000-unit monthly export threshold simultaneously, signalling that the overseas boom is no longer the exclusive property of two brands. Chery's formal acquisition of a factory on African soil and BYD's record share of overseas revenue underline that this is no longer just about shipping cars—it is about building durable industrial footholds abroad.

BYD: Record Exports, but Europe Localization Still Lagging

BYD posted 175,349 overseas units in June, a historic monthly record up 94.7% year-on-year and representing 43% of total group sales—the second consecutive month above the 40% threshold. [ElectricCarsReport] First-half cumulative overseas deliveries reached 792,256 units (+70.7%), putting the company at 52.8% of its upwardly revised full-year target of 1.5 million units. [CleanTechnica]

In Germany, the Atto 2 DM-i topped the PHEV segment in May and the Seal U DM-i repeated the feat in June, lifting BYD into the top three of Germany's hybrid market overall. [Ad-hoc-news] Yet the Hungary greenfield plant has been delayed to Q4 2026—roughly one year behind the original schedule—while brownfield acquisitions in Spain and France remain under evaluation. That means BYD will enter the second half of 2026 with zero European local production capacity, a significant geopolitical exposure given ongoing EU tariff deliberations. [AutomotiveWorld]

On the global BEV scorecard, BYD shipped 557,090 pure-electric vehicles in Q2, ahead of Tesla's 480,126, though BYD's BEV volume declined roughly 8% year-on-year while Tesla grew 25%, narrowing the gap. [Ad-hoc-news]

Chery: Africa Manufacturing Breakthrough and Another Monthly Record

Chery's June export figure of 191,062 units (+79.7%) was the fourth consecutive monthly record for any Chinese brand, bringing its H1 total to 943,817 units (+71.5%). [Gasgoo] The most structurally significant event of the week, however, was the formal completion on July 3rd of Chery's acquisition of the former Nissan Rosslyn plant in South Africa, retaining all 692 workers and launching local supply-chain sourcing, supporting approximately 3,000 additional jobs. [News18a]

In Europe, Chery's H1 sales reached 138,843 units (+216.7%), with NEV registrations in the region hitting 68,949 units in the first five months (+458.6%)—NEVs now account for 50% of Chery's European mix. The JAECOO 7 topped UK monthly sales charts, and the Jetour T2 won South Africa's Car of the Year award. [News18a] Chery has set a full-year overseas target of 1.5 million units with a 65–70% NEV mix; at 49.6% completion through H1, the second half will require sustained monthly volumes above 93,000 units. [SCMP]

Geely, Changan, and GAC: The New 100k Club

Geely broke the 100,000-unit monthly export barrier for the first time in June, delivering 102,874 units overseas (+157% YoY), with H1 cumulative exports of 474,228 units already surpassing the whole of 2025. [AllWeatherFinance] Zeekr delivered 178,370 units globally in H1 (+97%), captured 40.7% of Hong Kong's luxury EV segment, and held the top spot for pure-electric MPVs in Thailand for five consecutive months. [EVs&Beyond] The Galaxy Starship 7 EM-i has reached 57 countries, including topping Croatia's PHEV chart in its debut month. [AllWeatherFinance] Proton plans to begin producing the Zeekr 7X in Malaysia in early 2027, a move that analysts at Jefferies estimate could lift Geely's overseas gross margin roughly 10 percentage points above domestic levels. [KR-Asia]

Changan's H1 overseas volume reached 402,000 units, covering 53.6% of its full-year 750,000-unit target; June alone accounted for 91,000 units, or more than 45% of monthly total sales. [BigGo Finance] Its Rayong factory in Thailand has commenced production of the Deepal S05, with the first 500 units already shipped to the UK. Deepal S05 has placed in the top tier of compact pure-electric SUV sales in Spain for two consecutive months and formally entered Poland in May. [AutobaseCN]

GAC reported H1 exports of 121,483 units (+132%) in a press release issued July 9th, approaching its entire 2025 annual export total. [PRNewswire] The AION UT has entered local production in Austria and launched in the UK and Spain, while the model is among the top-ten BEV sellers in Mexico and ranked second among BEV brands in Singapore in April. [DailyWebPoint]

Leapmotor: Italy's EV Market in One Statistic

Leapmotor delivered a record 93,376 units globally in June (+94.5% YoY), bringing its H1 total to 356,487 units; overseas deliveries in H1 reached approximately 100,000 units, already exceeding the brand's full-year 2025 export figure. [Gasgoo] In Europe, Q1 registrations across 16 countries totalled 23,300 units (+726.5% YoY). The standout figure: Leapmotor now holds a 34.5% share of Italy's pure-electric market, rising to 48% in private-buyer channels—approximately one in every three EVs sold privately in Italy is a Leapmotor. [Gasgoo] Local production is being prepared in Spain, Malaysia, and Mexico, and the A10 city car is planned for European launch in late 2026 or early 2027. [Gasgoo]

SAIC, GWM, and Dongfeng: Manufacturing Responses to Tariff Pressure

SAIC confirmed plans to build MG's first European factory in Spain with an initial investment of approximately €200 million, targeting annual capacity of 120,000 units—a direct response to the EU's 37.6% countervailing duty, the highest applied to any Chinese automaker. [CnEVPost] The new MG 07, the brand's first LiDAR-equipped model, will open pre-sales in China on July 29th at an estimated ¥150,000–200,000. The Zhengzhou base that produced it simultaneously passed the 3-million-vehicle cumulative output milestone. [CnEVPost]

GWM reported 60,168 overseas units in June (+50.2%), and for the first time in H1 history, overseas volume (291,426 units) marginally exceeded domestic sales (292,469 units). [CnEVPost] The company's Q1 gross margin of 18.45% remains among the highest in the Chinese industry even as domestic volume slid 21.4%. [BigGo Finance]

Dongfeng's premium NEV brand Voyah delivered 76,264 units in H1 (+36% YoY) across 40-plus countries through 240-plus overseas outlets, and has signed a 51/49 European joint venture with Stellantis to handle sales and distribution on the continent, with local production at Stellantis's Rennes plant in France under discussion. [CarNewsChina] Dongfeng's "Sailing Plan," unveiled at the Hong Kong Motor Show on June 18th, pledges more than ¥100 billion in investment over five years to move from exports to local production to full overseas industrial ecosystems. [WBOC]

What It Means

The H1 2026 data confirm a structural inflection: China's overseas auto push has definitively moved beyond its BYD-and-Chery duopoly. At least five groups—BYD, Chery, Geely, Changan, and Leapmotor—are now operating at or near the 100,000-unit monthly export scale, and the fastest-moving actors are no longer content to export from Chinese factories alone. Chery's Rosslyn acquisition, GAC's Austrian assembly, SAIC's Spanish factory commitment, and Changan's Thai launch represent four distinct approaches to manufacturing localisation, each calibrated to a different tariff and regulatory environment. The common thread is urgency: with EU countervailing duties now applied and the Hungary greenfield still dark, every month of delay in local production is a month of compressed margin. For overseas buyers, dealers, and analysts, the key question for H2 is not whether Chinese brands can sell—they demonstrably can—but whether the localisation infrastructure can be built fast enough to protect the profitability that makes the entire expansion sustainable.

2026年上半年，中国汽车出口进入全新结构性阶段：比亚迪、奇瑞、吉利单月海外销量同步突破10万辆大关，标志着出口浪潮已远非两强独大的旧格局。奇瑞正式落地非洲制造、比亚迪海外收入占比创历史新高，共同印证一个判断：这场扩张已不再只是"装船出口"，而是在海外构建可持续的产业根基。

比亚迪：出口纪录再破，欧洲本地化仍是短板

比亚迪6月海外销量达175,349辆，同比增长94.7%，创历史单月纪录，海外占总销量43%，连续第二个月超过40%。[ElectricCarsReport] 上半年累计海外交付792,256辆（+70.7%），全年上调后的150万辆目标完成率达52.8%。[CleanTechnica]

德国市场，Atto 2 DM-i 5月领跑PHEV细分，Seal U DM-i 6月重复此战绩，比亚迪整体跻身德国混动市场前三。[Ad-hoc-news] 然而，匈牙利绿地工厂已延至2026年Q4投产，较原计划推迟约一年，西班牙及法国棕地收购方案仍在评估中。这意味着比亚迪将在欧洲本地产能为零的状态下进入下半年，在欧盟关税终裁悬而未决之际，地缘政治风险敞口依然较大。[AutomotiveWorld]

在全球纯电榜单上，比亚迪Q2纯电发货557,090辆，超越特斯拉的480,126辆，但比亚迪BEV同比约下滑8%，特斯拉同期增长25%，两者差距正在收窄。[Ad-hoc-news]

奇瑞：落地非洲制造，月度出口再创纪录

奇瑞6月出口191,062辆（+79.7%），第四个月连续刷新中国品牌单月出口纪录，上半年累计出口达943,817辆（+71.5%）。[Gasgoo] 本周最具结构性意义的事件，是奇瑞于7月3日正式完成对南非罗斯林工厂（前日产工厂）的收购，保留全部692名员工，带动约3,000个供应链就业岗位，本地化采购同步启动。[News18a]

欧洲方面，奇瑞上半年销量达138,843辆（+216.7%），前5个月新能源车注册量68,949辆（+458.6%），NEV已占欧洲销量的50%。JAECOO 7摘得英国单月销量冠军，捷途T2荣获南非年度车大奖。[News18a] 奇瑞全年海外目标为150万辆，NEV占比目标65%至70%；上半年完成率仅49.6%，下半年月均需维持9.3万辆以上方能达标，压力不小。[SCMP]

吉利、长安、广汽：共同跨入"10万辆俱乐部"

吉利6月海外出口首破10万辆里程碑，单月交付102,874辆（+157%），上半年累计474,228辆，已超过2025全年出口总量。[AllWeatherFinance] 极氪上半年全球交付178,370辆（+97%），在香港奢华电动车市场占据40.7%份额，连续5个月蝉联泰国纯电MPV销量冠军。[EVs&Beyond] 银河星舰7 EM-i已进入57个国家和地区，并在克罗地亚首月即夺PHEV销量冠军。[AllWeatherFinance] 宝腾计划于2027年初在马来西亚开始生产极氪7X；据Jefferies估计，吉利海外业务毛利率比国内高出约10个百分点。[KR-Asia]

长安上半年海外销量达402,000辆，完成全年75万辆目标的53.6%；6月单月出口91,000辆，占当月总销量45%以上。[BigGo Finance] 泰国罗勇工厂已正式投产，深蓝S05首批500辆已发往英国，该车型在西班牙已连续两个月跻身紧凑纯电SUV销量前列，并于5月正式进入波兰市场。[AutobaseCN]

广汽于7月9日发布官方新闻稿，公布上半年出口121,483辆（+132%），已接近2025全年出口总量。[PRNewswire] 埃安UT已在奥地利开始本地生产并进入英国和西班牙市场，同时跻身墨西哥纯电销量前十，4月在新加坡位列新能源品牌第二。[DailyWebPoint]

零跑：用意大利市场的一组数字说明一切

零跑6月全球交付创纪录的93,376辆（+94.5%），上半年累计356,487辆；海外上半年交付约10万辆，已超过2025全年出口总量。[Gasgoo] 欧洲方面，Q1在16国注册23,300辆（+726.5%）。最亮眼的数字是：零跑目前占据意大利纯电市场34.5%的份额，私人购车渠道更高达48%——意大利每3辆私人购买的纯电车中，就有1辆以上是零跑。[Gasgoo] 西班牙、马来西亚、墨西哥的本地生产布局正在推进，A10微型车计划于2026年底至2027年初进入欧洲市场。[Gasgoo]

上汽、长城、东风：以制造本地化回应关税压力

上汽确认将在西班牙建设MG首座欧洲工厂，初始投资约2亿欧元，规划年产能12万辆，旨在规避欧盟对其征收的37.6%反补贴税——这是所有中国车企中最高的税率。[CnEVPost] 全新MG 07搭载LiDAR，将于7月29日在中国开启预售，预计售价15至20万元；郑州基地同日完成第300万辆下线里程碑。[CnEVPost]

长城汽车6月海外销量60,168辆（+50.2%），上半年海外销量（291,426辆）首次历史性地超过国内销量（292,469辆）。[CnEVPost] Q1毛利率18.45%，在国内同比下滑21.4%的背景下，海外业务已成为唯一增长引擎。[BigGo Finance]

东风旗下高端新能源品牌岚图上半年全球交付76,264辆（+36%），覆盖40余个国家和地区、240余个海外销售网点，并与Stellantis签署51/49欧洲销售合资意向书，探索在Stellantis法国雷恩工厂本地化生产岚图车型。[CarNewsChina] 东风"扬帆计划"于6月18日香港车展发布，承诺5年内投入逾1,000亿元，推进从产品出口到本地化生产再到海外产业生态的三阶段国际化战略。[WBOC]

战略解读

2026年上半年数据确认了一个结构性拐点：中国汽车出口已彻底告别"比亚迪+奇瑞"双雄垄断的旧格局。比亚迪、奇瑞、吉利、长安、零跑至少五家集团已同步运行在月度出口10万辆的量级，而最具进取心的玩家，早已不甘于仅仅从中国工厂装船出货。奇瑞收购罗斯林、广汽奥地利组装、上汽西班牙建厂承诺、长安泰国工厂投产——这四条路径代表四种截然不同的本地化制造逻辑，各自针对不同的关税与监管环境而设计。共同的主线只有一条：紧迫。欧盟反补贴税已落地，匈牙利工厂仍一片沉寂，本地产能每推迟一个月，就是一个月的利润压缩。对于海外买家、经销商和分析师而言，下半年的核心问题已不再是中国品牌能不能卖——这已有充分证明——而是本地化制造基础设施能否以足够快的速度建立起来，从而保护支撑整个扩张逻辑的盈利能力。
